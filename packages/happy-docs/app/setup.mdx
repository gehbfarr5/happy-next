## Pair with your machine

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a Happy session on your computer">
    Run `happy` in your terminal. A QR code will appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Happy app">
    Launch the app on your phone. On first launch, you'll be guided through account setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Tap the scan icon on the home screen and point your camera at the QR code in your terminal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connected">
    Your phone is now paired. You'll see your active session in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account

Happy uses **cryptographic authentication** — no passwords. Your identity is a key pair generated on your device. When you scan the QR code, your phone and computer exchange public keys to establish a secure connection.

## Settings

Access settings by tapping the gear icon. Key options:

- **Appearance** — Light, dark, or automatic theme
- **Voice** — Voice provider and language settings
- **Notifications** — Push notification preferences
- **AI Backend Profiles** — Configure custom API endpoints for Claude, Codex, or Gemini
- **GitHub** — Connect your GitHub account for social features
