Happy Next is a mobile companion for [Claude Code](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/claude-code), [Codex](https://openai.com/index/codex/), and [Gemini CLI](https://github.com/google-gemini/gemini-cli). It lets you monitor and control your AI coding sessions from anywhere — your phone, tablet, or another computer.

## Why Happy Next?

AI coding agents run long tasks. You shouldn't have to sit at your desk watching them. Happy gives you:

- **Mobile control** — Send instructions, approve permissions, and review code from your phone
- **Real-time sync** — See what your agent is doing right now, across all your devices
- **Voice control** — Talk to your AI agent instead of typing
- **Multi-agent support** — Claude Code, Codex, and Gemini in one place
- **End-to-end encryption** — Your code and conversations are encrypted before leaving your device. We can't read them.
- **Task orchestration** — Dispatch multi-agent workflows with dependency graphs

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the CLI">
    Install the CLI on your development machine. It wraps your existing AI coding tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a session">
    Run `happy` to start a Claude Code session (or `happy codex` / `happy gemini`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your phone">
    Scan the QR code with the Happy mobile app to pair your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Code from anywhere">
    Send messages, review code, and approve permissions — all from your phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>
